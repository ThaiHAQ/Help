## Help for Thai BASI app

**Main Feature**

• Create database to keep questionnaire's record.<br />
• Export database to Excel sheet.<br /> 
• Print questionnaire into PDF format.<br /> 

**Instruction**

• The objective of this questionnaire is to evaluate disease activity in patients who have been diagnosed with spondyloarthritis.<br /> 
• It is NOT for providing diagnosis or considering treatment option.<br />
